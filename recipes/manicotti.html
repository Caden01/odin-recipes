Manicotti

Description

Manicotti is an italian dish with large and tubular pasta shells that are stuffed. Manicotti is traditionally with a chees mixture and tomato sauce. "Manicotti" litearlly means "little sleeves"

Ingredients

  • 5 1/2 ounces manicotti pasta
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1(16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
